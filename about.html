Discover the heart and soul of Our Mother of Perpetual Help Redemptorist Church

Our Story

Our Mother of Perpetual Help Redemptorist Church has been a beacon of faith and community in Dumaguete City since 1974. Founded by the Redemptorist Fathers, our parish has grown from a small congregation to a vibrant community of believers committed to living out the Gospel message.

Named after Our Lady of Perpetual Help, our patroness, the church serves as a spiritual home for thousands of families, providing sacraments, education, and charitable works that reflect Christ's love for all people.

Our History

A timeline of faith, service, and community growth

1974

Parish Founded

Our Mother of Perpetual Help Redemptorist Church was established by the Redemptorist Fathers as a mission parish in Dumaguete City, responding to the growing Catholic community in the area.

1980

First Church Building

The first church structure was completed, serving as a place of worship and community gathering for the growing congregation.

1995

School Ministry Begins

Parish-based religious education programs were established, providing catechetical formation for children and adults in our community.

2025

Digital Parish System

Launch of our comprehensive online parish management system, enabling parishioners to access services, make requests, and stay connected digitally.
