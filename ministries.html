Our Ministries

Discover ways to serve God and community through our various ministry programs

Serve with Purpose

Our ministries provide opportunities for every parishioner to use their unique gifts and talents in service to God and our community. Whether through worship, education, charity, or fellowship, there's a place for you to make a difference.

"For we are God's handiwork, created in Christ Jesus to do good works, which God prepared in advance for us to do." - Ephesians 2:10
