About Our Parish

Our Mother of Perpetual Help Redemptorist Church has been serving the Dumaguete community for over 50 years, providing spiritual guidance, education, and support to all parishioners.

We are committed to fostering a welcoming environment where faith and fellowship thrive, following the Redemptorist tradition of compassionate service and devotion to Our Lady.
